Who Dr. Norman Borlaug Was

Dr. Norman Borlaug, often referred to as the "Father of the Green Revolution," dedicated his life to combating world hunger through agricultural innovation. His pioneering research in plant breeding and development of high-yielding, disease-resistant wheat varieties transformed agriculture and saved millions from starvation.
